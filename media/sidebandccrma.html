About Sideband
PLOrk Sideband is an ever-evolving professional offshoot of the Princeton Laptop Orchestra, comprised of composers and performers who have come in contact with PLOrk in their academic and professional musical lives but are no longer students at Princeton. Many great works have come out of the group thus far.
